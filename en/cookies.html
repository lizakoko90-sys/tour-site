1. What cookies are

Cookies are small text files that a website stores in the User's browser. They help the website function properly, remember the User's choices and collect anonymised visit statistics.

2. What cookies we use

  • Technical (necessary) — ensure the basic operation of the website: for example, remembering that consent to the use of cookies has been given.
  • Analytical — help us understand how visitors use the website (pages visited, referral sources) in order to improve the service. They may be set by third-party web analytics services.
  • Functional — remember the User's preferences for convenience.

3. Purposes of use

Ensuring the website's functionality, analysing traffic, and improving the convenience and quality of the service.

4. How to manage cookies

The User may at any time delete stored cookies and prevent them from being set through their browser settings. Please note: disabling certain cookies may affect the correct operation of the website.

5. Consent

By continuing to use the website and/or clicking the "Accept" button in the information banner, the User consents to the use of cookies in accordance with this Policy. For more details on data processing, see the Privacy Policy.
